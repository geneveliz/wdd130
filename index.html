Venezuela

Tachira is one of the states of Venezuela, it is located in the west of the country, in the Andes region.It borders to the north with Zulia, to the northeast with Mérida, to the south with Barinas and Apure, and to the west with the Republic of Colombia. It is made up of 29 autonomous municipalities and 66 civil parishes. Among its main cities are San Cristóbal, Táriba, Rubio, La Grita, San Antonio del Táchira, La Fría, Santa Ana del Táchira, Capacho Nuevo, San Juan de Colón, Cordero and Capacho Viejo.
